I'm Jimiel Tomlin, a Professional Wrestling Photographer devoted to capturing moments that bring the art of storytelling to life. With over five years of experience, I focus on the intensity, drama, and raw emotion that make wrestling an unforgettable spectacle.

My passion for pro wrestling runs deep – it’s a world where every match is a story, every move has meaning, and each wrestler has a character they embody with heart and dedication. I love the energy, the personalities, and the unscripted moments that can turn a match into a masterpiece. Through my lens, I aim to preserve these powerful, fleeting moments that reveal the soul of wrestling.

When I’m not ringside, you’ll find me exploring new places, coding, or editing photos. For me, photography is more than technical skill – it's a way to share the thrill of wrestling with fans everywhere, capturing the passion and drama that make this sport unlike any other on earth.
